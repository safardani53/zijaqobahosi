'FBoy Island' Season 3: Everything to Know

 Posted on August 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  225 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

FBoy Island is getting a second life on The CW after Max canceled the series following the release of season 2.

“With its innovative and modern twist on the reality dating genre, incredibly talented host Nikki Glaser and truly unforgettable title, FBoy Island on The CW is a perfect match,” Heather Olander, Head of Unscripted Content for The CW, said in a May 2023 statement.

FBoy Island follows three women in paradise joined by 26 men, half of whom are self-proclaimed “Nice Guys” looking for love and half of whom are self-proclaimed “Fboys” seeking only a cash prize. The women must try to distinguish the sincere from the sneaky as they pursue romantic connections with the men.

Despite the show’s move to The CW, Glaser is set to continue her role as host and executive producer. “I’m so damn excited that my favorite reality TV show is coming back,” she said in a statement that May. “It’s icing on the cake that I get to host it again. I hope I forget everything that happens while I make the show so that I can relive it all when it airs. I couldn’t be prouder to have my name on such a hilarious, captivating and ridiculous show.”
