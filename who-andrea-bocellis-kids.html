ITALIAN opera singer, Andrea Bocelli, is globally recognized for his iconic tenor vocals.

After years in the spotlight alone, the 64-year-old is finally getting to share the stage with his own talented children.

Who are Andrea Bocelli’s kids?

Andrea Bocelli is one of the most well-known singers around the world.

After becoming completely blind at the age of 12, Bocelli rose to fame in 1994 following his win at the 44th Sanremo Music Festival.

Today, he has the honor of sharing his fame with his three children.

Amos & Matteo Bocelli

Amos and Matteo were born through Bocelli's first marriage to Enrica Cenzatti.

The pair tied the knot back in 1992 and welcomed their first son, Amos, in 1995, and their second, Matteo, in 1997.

The couple separated five years later.

Both Amos and Matteo have followed in their father's musical footsteps.

Amos, 27, is a gifted pianist who, in 2016, became a member of the board of directors of his father’s charity initiative, ABF Andrea Bocelli Foundation.

Matteo, 25, is a singer, like his father, and the pair have released a number of duets together.

“Technically they are very different, but there is one thing that makes them most similar: the attitude. That characteristic way of expressing yourself through your voice,” Bocelli told Billboard in 2018 of the blending of his voice with his son’s in their track, Fall On Me.

“This makes Matteo and I very similar. But that is something that you cannot learn, that cannot be taught. That is something you are born with.”

Virginia Bocelli

Virginia was born through Bocelli's second and current marriage to Veronica Berti.

The pair reportedly met back in 2002 and officially tied the knot in 2014.

Virginia, however, was born back in 2012.

The talented 10-year-old is often found singing alongside her father, and brother, Matteo, on grand stages all around the world.
