How to Choose Quality Fishnet Tights?

When you purchase a pair of fishnet stockings, it is important that they last and do not become loose or tear after one or two wears. There are several things you should keep in mind when you are browsing for a pair.

Material

The material of fishnet stockings is an important consideration because that will decide how comfortable and stretchable they are. Most tights are made from nylon because it is a soft material and paired with spandex, it provides sufficient elasticity. Make sure you buy a pair that does not become loose after wearing them a few times.

Color

The color is also an important consideration. Most tights come in black color because they are suitable for most outfits, but you should consider colors that match your skin tone as well. These are gaining popularity gradually, and you can find several color options provided by different brands.

Weave size

The size of the weave can have a huge impact on how the tights look on you. In our opinion, you should invest in a pack that includes three to four pieces, each having a different weave size. This way you can mix and match different styles according to different outfits. Most women prefer a medium-sized weave, while larger weaves are a little less common.

Size

The size is the most important consideration because you want to make sure that they fit you well. Fishnet tights are meant to fit snugly, but they should be comfortable as well. A lot of brands provide a universal fit that is supposed to fit every woman, but sometimes these will not fit plus-sized women. So, it is important that you check the dimensions of the length and waist sizes before you purchase one.

How to Wash Fishnet Tights?

Washing fishnet tights can be a little tricky because they can easily tear or get damaged even if mishandled slightly. We would advise you to hand wash your tights, even if the brand recommends a machine wash. This is because they can easily get tangled in the washing machine. The first step you want to do is to turn your tights inside out. This will protect the outer design. A combination of mild detergent and lukewarm water is ideal for washing fishnet stockings by hand. You can simply wash them in the sink or use a tub. Rinse them with warm water after submerging them in the mixture of detergent and water. You do not need to rub them too much, or you can damage them. Do not even wring them too harshly, and hang them out to dry. Do not wear them until they are completely dry.

If you do want to use a machine to wash them, make sure that you use a gentle and cold cycle. Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach and fabric softener. Do not wash them with other pieces of clothing, especially with fabrics that can produce lint. Also, avoid using the dryer and let them air dry to increase their life.

How to Style Fishnet Stockings?

Fishnet stockings can be tricky to style, even though they can be worn in both casual and formal environments. When styling them for a casual hangout, you can wear them under shorts and mini skirts and wear a casual tee or crop top. This is ideal for a day out in the summers. However, when you want to wear them to a formal function like a work meeting, make sure you wear a skirt or dress of an appropriate length. Wearing a mini skirt that exposes your legs too much can be considered inappropriate. You can even wear them under ripped and distressed jeans for a unique look. When going to a party, you can use your imagination. A black skirt on black fishnet tights is usually a good look. It is important that you coordinate your shoes accordingly as well. Heels and boots go well with them.
