The 13 Best Work Bags for Women of 2024

 Posted on July 24, 2024  |  19 minutes  |  3918 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Any coffee shop commuter or office-goer would agree that a work bag is more than just a bag; the difference between a mediocre and great one is, in fact, significant. When you tote around a bag for work, it needs to be durable, easy to organize, able to protect your laptop, and also have enough room to fit all your day’s essentials — and perhaps even be versatile enough to bring with you for post-work activities, whether that's a workout class or happy hour.

“One of the biggest trends I'm seeing regarding work bags is overall versatility of use,” says celebrity stylist Chelsea Volpe. “For example, many working women are using the same bag for their morning workout, office bag, and even transitioning it into their evening bag. A medium to full-size leather tote is a perfect bag to transition from the gym to the office to a happy hour cocktail with friends.”

To start our search, we researched popular work bags on the market, spoke with experts, pored over reviews, and tested most of them ourselves, to ultimately align on this list of the best work bags for women.

Keep reading to discover PEOPLE's picks, as well as more expert shopping advice.

How to Pick the Right Work Bag

Durability

“Another important consideration is material and durability,” says celebrity stylist Chelsea Volpe. “A cloth bag won't survive a morning coffee spill or torrential downpour en route to the office. Consider materials like leather, which is luxe, durable and looks elevated for an important meeting with your boss. Another great material is polyester, which you can often throw in the wash or hand clean with a basic soft cloth. Polyester and other synthetic materials are also less expensive than the more upscale leather bag for the budget conscious buyer." Additionally, a waxed or water-resistant bag (like the Quince Revive Nylon Large Tote) is a great durable option for your everyday commute.

Functionality

"Look for a bag with a high drool factor that can fit your everyday essentials and accommodate your daily commuting needs," says Conroy. "Pick a structured silhouette with a flat base. Both will offer stability, so your work bag doesn’t tip over and spill its contents — or worse, damage your laptop. Make a beeline for bags with protective feet, divided compartments, and zip pouches for securing your small essentials."

Additionally, think of features you’ll need specifically on your commute and at the office. Are you someone who takes your water bottle everywhere? Then you should look for a work bag that includes easy access to your water bottle, like the Freja New York Paloma Tote or the Quince Revive Nylon Large Tote, just to name a few.

Size and Shape

Volpe explains that size is an important factor, as well. “A work bag that is too large will be difficult to schlep on your morning commute, however something too compact won't fit for example your laptop,” she says. “A medium-size, well-compartmentalized bag with a laptop sleeve is always a win.” Some medium-size bags with plenty of compartments and a laptop sleeve include the Béis The Work Tote and the Calpak Haven Laptop Bag.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Which work bag is the best?

    It mostly depends on what your needs are, but if you are wanting a timeless style that's also great for travel, ewe recommend the Béis The Work Tote because of its versatility, functionality, sleek look, and reasonable price point. The Lululemon Side-Cinch Shopper Bag is a great everyday option as it boasts a casual feel and can be worn from the gym to the office.

  • Are expensive work bags worth it?

    Typically, bags that are more expensive are made with higher quality materials, but not always. Sometimes you’re paying for the extra storage and functional bells and whistles, or you could just be paying for the brand name.

    "Delivering equal measures of fashion and function, expensive work bags are often worth the indulgence: Better construction, higher end materials and thicker straps that can withstand all of the heavy goodies you stash in your bag," explains Conroy. "But sometimes expensive 'it bags' of the season miss the mark on performance. So, shop with care. Like dating, don’t fall in love with a sexy counterfeit. Make sure your roomy carryall doesn’t just look good, it meets your wish list. The bag of your dreams might cost you much less than you think."

    There are plenty of other great work bags on the list that are much more affordable and still offer plenty of functionality and durability as well. "Budget-friendly work bags don’t have to be hideous," says Conroy. "Dabble with brands like Béis or Denmark’s very own Rains to fully complete the affordable artisan experience."

  • Do work bags need to be washable?

    It’s not essential for work bags to be easily washable, although that’s one perk for our machine-washable pick: Rothy’s The Lightweight Tote. If it’s not machine-washable, though, make sure the bag is still easy to spot clean and is durable enough to resist the inevitable wear and tear that comes with toting around a large, heavy bag.
