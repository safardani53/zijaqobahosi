GWYNETH Paltrow's daughter, Apple, has stunned fans with her likeness to the star in new photos from a high-profile event.

The Goop founder does not often share her kids on social media.

Gwyneth, 50, and Apple, 19, attended an event in the Hamptons in New York that was hosted by Good, Gucci, and Elizabeth Saltzman.

The Iron Man star wore a floral print dress with white high heels.

Her daughter, meanwhile, kept it chic and simple in a black mini dress and matching heels.

The pair posted together at various photo opportunities at the event.

Gwyneth hasn't shared a photo of Apple online since May when she posted a sweet birthday tribute to her daughter.

She is also mom to a son named Moses, 17.

The wellness mogul shares both kids with ex-husband Chris Martin.

DEVOTED MOM

Despite rarely featuring them online, it's clear that Gwyneth is a devoted mom to her kids.

When fans do get glimpses at the kids, they're often shocked at how grown-up they appear.

In 2022, the actress shared a newsletter that featured a photo of her and both of her kids together.

A Chris and Gwyneth fan page on Instagram shared a screengrab of the pic online.

The screenshot showed Apple and Moses posing for the picture. 

Apple was pulling a serious face as she held up a peace sign while Moses' hair was swept in front of his eyes with his mouth is open. 

The children set the photo as their mother's lock screen.

Fans were quick to comment on how much her children have grown up. 

"Wow... they grew up so handsome and beautiful," wrote one fan at the time. 

GWYN'S MAN

Following her "conscious uncoupling" from Chris, Gwyneth met and later married Brad Falchuk.

Brad is a television writer, director, and producer who is best known for his work on Glee and American Horror Story.

The couple first met in 2010 and went public with their romance in 2015.

He and Gwyneth married in September 2018 in a celebrity-packed ceremony in The Hamptons.

The wedding took place in the garden of a private home in the exclusive resort beach town.
