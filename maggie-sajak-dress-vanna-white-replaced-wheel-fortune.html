MAGGIE Sajak, the daughter of Wheel of Fortune host Pat Sajak, has stunned in a teeny tiny dress amid game show changes.

Maggie, 28, shared a steamy snapshot of herself dressed in pink. 

In the Instagram photo, Maggie teased her long legs while modeling a bright pink dress with a short skirt. 

The social media correspondent for Wheel of Fortune flaunted her slender frame as she displayed cleavage from her plunging top. 

She posed on a white sofa while crossing her bare legs.

Maggie captioned the post: "Mentally, I am (still) here."

In the comments section, a fan gushed: "Maggie, have you ever thought about flipping letters as in permanent job?! You would be so good!"

MISSING OUT

The fan's comment comes as longtime Wheel of Fortune star Vanna, 66, is in the midst of unresolved contract negotiations to get higher pay.

In July, the TV star hired high-powered lawyer Bryan Friedman to negotiate two deals - one for Celebrity Wheel of Fortune and the other for the long-running syndicated show, according to TMZ. 

Vanna's upcoming absence from the show comes amid the tense contract negations for her first reported raise in 18 years.

On August 11, Puck News reported Vanna will miss a few episodes of Wheel of Fortune this fall.

Vanna reportedly contracted COVID-19 in late July while the program was filming its annual Teachers Week, which is scheduled to air in October.

Instead of halting production, producers decided on a sub and contacted Teacher of the Year recipient Bridgette Donald-Blue.

Bridgette will replace Vanna on the letter board for the week of installments.

NEW HOST

More changes are coming to the iconic game show as Pat, 76, announced his retirement after 40 years of hosting the program.

In June, the Wheel of Fortune announced via Instagram that Ryan Seacrest, 48, will take over for Pat at the end of next season.

Ryan confirmed Vanna will remain on the puzzle board in September 2024.

He said: "I can say, along with the rest of America, that it's been a privilege and pure joy to watch Pat and Vanna on our television screens for an unprecedented 40 years.

"I can't wait to continue the tradition of spinning the wheel and working alongside the great Vanna White."

SEEING MORE OF MAGGIE?

As the game show deals with its behind-the-scenes drama, fans have been clamoring for Maggie to take on a bigger role in the program. 

On two occasions, Maggie took on puzzle duties while Vanna was still on-screen.
