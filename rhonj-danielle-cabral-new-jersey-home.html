Inside RHONJ newbie Danielle Cabral's modest $340K NJ home as co-stars flaunt $3M mega-mansions

 Posted on June 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  394 words  |  Larita Shotwell

REAL Housewives of New Jersey star Danielle Cabral owns a modest New Jersey home she bought for $340,000, as her co-stars flaunt mega-mansions. 

Danielle, 37, joined The Real Housewives of New Jersey for season 13 with fellow full-time newcomer Rachel Fuda and friend Jennifer Fessler.

The U.S. Sun can reveal exclusive details about the stunning home she shares with her husband, Nathan, and their two children Dominic and Valentina. 

The couple purchased the Sayreville, New Jersey home for $340,000 in 2013.

The home boasts four bedrooms, three bathrooms and a two-car garage. 

According to real estate photos from 2013, the new Bravo stars made major renovations to the house.

The entrance now has bold black and white tile flooring. 

The kitchen boasts white cabinets with silver hardware, gray granite countertops, a kitchen island, a walk-in pantry and stainless steel appliances. 

The family room has hardwood floors.

The upstairs bathroom has beige and white tile walls with a mosaic band.

The master bedroom has two closets and a dressing area. 

The house also features a basement and an in-ground pool in the backyard. 

The home is currently estimated at $540,000, according to Zillow. 

'CUTE' ABODE

On this week’s episode, Danielle had her co-stars over for a mozzarella-making party. 

She said of her house: “I’m a little nervous hosting these girls in my house because Jennifer’s house can literally eat my house for breakfast, okay?

“I’m not saying my house isn’t nice, but if someone says my house is ‘cute,’ like small, I’m going to have a problem.”

When co-star Dolores Catania arrived to the abode, she said: “I love it here! So cute!”

Danielle said in her confessional: “Here we go.” 

CO-STARS HOMES

Danielle is close with stars Teresa Giudice and Jennifer Aydin, as the women often hang out and post photos together on social media. 

Teresa, 50, and her husband, Luis Ruelas, bought a $3,350,000 home in Montville, Township. 

The mansion boasts 10 bathrooms and seven bedrooms. 

Jennifer, 45, and her husband, Bill, live in a massive nine-bedroom, sixteen-bathroom home in Paramus, New Jersey that they bought for $1,075,000 in 2014, but is now estimated at $3.1million.
