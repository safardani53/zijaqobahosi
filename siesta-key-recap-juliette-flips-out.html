Siesta Key Recap: Juliette Flips OUT

 Posted on April 25, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  665 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Juliette and Alex have been screaming from the rooftops that their relationship is in good shape, but the cracks started to appear on Tuesday’s episode of Siesta Key. 

From them bickering over what Juliette would be doing post-college to another big blowout argument while out their friends, we think it’s fair to say these two are not going to stand the test of time. 

It all started because Alex said that Juliette’s dream to manage a Pure Barre was "not realistic."

Yes, relationships are built on communication, but Alex made sure to shoot down Juliette’s ambitions before they became a reality. 

While on a night out with their friends, the couple’s argument exploded and it was in full view of their friends and the other patrons of the bar. 

Juliette asked Alex to get her some red bull and pizza, and Alex’s ego started to get the best of him. 

"You literally have it so good," he said to a stunned Juliette before he started listing the things he does for her. 

Yeah, Alex is still the most obnoxious personality on this show, and when Juliette pointed out that his parents are very rich, he flew off the handle. 

"You are being a brat right now. You wanna walk through the things I’ve done for you today? Literally, I do everything for you," he complained.

"You need to stop pinning nice things you do for me on me like I owe you," Juliette retorted. "We’re in a relationship. Why do you have to start tallying the nice things you do for me?"

But then Alex stuck the knife in further by saying his girlfriend does absolutely "nothing" for him. 

Yeah, that’s what happens when she drives all weekend from college to come to visit your sorry self, bruh. 

Elsewhere, Chloe finally confronted Alex about the way everyone reacted to her bringing his ex, Cara, back into the fold. 

“I just never thought I was going to be on the outs with so many people in this group,” Chloe admitted to Alex. “These are all my best friends since I was like, 13/14 years old.”

Alex tried to defend Juliette, but Chloe did not hold back about her feelings on the matter. 

“Well I’m pissed at Juliette for everything she’s done to ME,” she claimed.

“She was totally like…I’ve done nothing to you. Obviously, if Juliette’s not going to own up to any of it then I’m not going to own up to any of it.”

Chloe went on to say that she was not trying to cause drama and that Cara was her friend. 

Then, the conversation turned emotional when Chloe dragged Alex for what he told Juliette. 

“You apparently decided to go and tell Juliette about how I used to be in love with you,” Chloe said while fighting back the tears. 

“I just am feeling really betrayed that you would bring up some sensitive subject. You know how much it took me to come to that, so I don’t even know why you would use this against me.”

They got into a heated debate, but Alex wanted to stop all of the arguing within the group. 

“You guys need to start talking,” the rich kid said of Chloe’s plight to mend fences with Juliette.

“You can talk to me all you want, but Juliette’s more sensitive than me, and you know that. There should be a way that we can all make this thing work," he continued. 

"It just seems like if you’re not pissing her off, she’s pissing you off. Here’s the bottom line: I’m putting together a trip to Miami for Juliette."

"If you want to go — you need to make things right with Juliette. If you don’t, then you can’t come to Miami. That’s the way it’s going to be.”
