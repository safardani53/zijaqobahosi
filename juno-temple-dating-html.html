Is 'Ted Lasso Star Juno Temple Dating Phil Dunster? Get to Know Her Personal Life

 Posted on July 6, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  506 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Many famous men have dated Juno Temple, but fans cannot help but wonder if she is dating her costar, Phil Dunster. 

Temple and Dunster starred as a couple for some time in AppleTV’s Emmy nominated series, Ted Lasso, which premiered in 2020 and became an instant hit. 

The former took on the role of Keeley Jones, while the latter portrayed Jamie Tartt, a lothario soccer player. 

On October 2021, the costars were spotted at Neiman Marcus’s Fantasy Gift event party at LA’s Paramount Studios. 

They hosted the event together, which led fans to wonder if they were together.

Juno Temple and Phil Dunster, who Temple was rumored to be dating at the Neiman Marcus event (Source: Juno Temple/Instagram)

But so far, nothing suggests the duo are together, and according to People, Dunster is currently in a relationship. 

The actor is currently with filmmaker Eleanor Hayden. 

“It should be known that she is truly amazing and the best person I know,” Dunster said of his girlfriend. 

As for Temple, it appears she is currently single unless she is dating someone secretly, which we are not aware of.

Juno Temple Dating Michael Angarano

Temple is very private about her love life, but her relationship with This is Us actor Michael Angarano was not. 

The BAFTA-nominated star and Angarano met on the set of 2012’s fantasy film, The Brass Teapot, and hit it off immediately. They started dating soon after.

According to an insider, the pair “hit it off and had great chemistry from the start.” 

“It was an intimate set and everyone really bonded. They were both single, and it just worked,” they added.

Glamour Magazine UK reported that the former couple also attended the Sundance Film Festival at the premiere of Temple’s new movie, Afternoon Delight.

The duo was spotted hand in hand as they cuddled and posed for photographers.

Angarano also joined her and the rest of the cast members for dinner.

An insider said that the actress “ran over to him, wrapped her arms around him and started kissing him,” before she left the premiere.

The source added that the actors tried to keep their relationship private. 

Juno Temple and Her past Relationships

They supported each other, especially Angarano, who helped Temple relieve some of her stress from all the newfound fame. 

In 2014, the couple moved in together, but by 2016 they called it quits, and no one knows why. 

After Angarano, Temple moved on with writer and producer Joshua Thurston.

The couple started dating from late 2016 to 2017.

The actress still has a picture with her former boyfriend from October 2017 on her Instagram. 

The actress had uploaded the photo in honor of Thurston’s birthday, which features Thurston sleeping on her lap. 

According to reports, the actress also dated Shiloh Thomas Fernandez, best known for his roles in the 2013 remake of Evil Dead from August 2008 to 2010. 

She was also rumored to have hooked up with Killer Joe costar Emile Hirsch in November 2010, but it has never been confirmed. 

However, with that being said, the notoriously private actress has never confirmed or denied these relationship speculations.
