Drake target shirt: How to get unofficial For All The Dogs merch

 Posted on June 6, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  493 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

DRAKE fans have been flocking to Target to pick up unofficial For All The Dogs merch after a video went viral on TikTok.

But unfortunately, shoppers won't find the graphic T-shirts at the retailer.

What is the Drake Target shirt?

Since Drake released For All The Dogs, fans have been eagerly awaiting a merch drop.

But an unofficial graphic tee on display at a Target store has put shoppers' heads in a spin.

A video was posted on TikTok showing a T-shirt with Drake's side profile printed on the front.

Another had the iconic children's drawing of a goat that can be seen on the album cover.

But the overlay text on the item: “Drake fans need to run to Target ASAP”, is misleading.

Where can you buy the Drake For All The Dogs shirt?

The TikTok account dedicated to the clothing store Eternal Iconz has created the graphic tees inspired by For All The Dogs and is expected to be released on December 8.

While the item should be available on the brand’s online store, it won’t be sold at Target.

The content creator claims in the comments that the item is displayed in the store only for the sake of promotion.

Eternal Iconz took to Instagram to announce to customers that they will be giving away free t-shirts to the top three "most active" in the comment section.

Why has the Drake For All The Dogs shirt gone viral?

Drake fans have been awaiting their For All The Dogs collectibles since the album dropped on October 6, 2023.

On YouTube, the full album has racked up over eight million views and the album has surpassed 1.2billion streams on Spotify.

So the news of merch being available in Target stores sent fans on a mad dash to get theirs - but they were left disappointed.

“This has me going to the online app to find it,” commented one shopper under the fake TikTok video.

“It took me so long to read the caption and realize they aren’t sold at Target. sigh,” added another.

A third fan wrote: “Don’t you ever play with my feelings like that! I need this.”

“I went to Target after seeing this and the shirt wasn’t there,” read one comment.

The white t-shirt by Eternal Iconz depicts a picture of Drake in colorful hair clips.

The rapper sported the unique hairdo on multiple occasions ahead of the release of his album and is carrying the same look in the music video of 8AM in Charlotte.

It even inspired some Halloween costumes this year.

Another shirt showcases the picture of a goat drawn by his young son.

On the back are graphic images of Drake below the title of the album.

The clothing brand hasn’t revealed the price of the item, but most t-shirts on the website inspired by other famous musicians and sports stars are all sold for $49.99.
