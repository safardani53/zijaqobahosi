SHOPPERS don’t have to splurge to achieve the iconic Free People look, as one dupe shirt is available for just $24 on Amazon.

Free People is a top-tier women’s bohemian fashion store located at malls across the country.

While the retailer has achieved a considerable amount of popularity, shoppers can easily fork over nearly $100 for one shirt alone.

Some dresses, meanwhile, go for more than $200, making the store out of reach for those with a smaller budget.

However, shoppers still have hope if they want to implement the iconic Free People style, as one savvy shopper found a perfect dupe of the brand for just $24 on Amazon.

The item in question, the Intercept Pullover, sells on Free People’s website for a total of $78.

But you don’t have to dip into your savings to secure the baggy, aesthetic look.

With a little digging, you’ll find that Amazon’s Amazhiyu Women Cropped Sweatshirt Long Sleeves Pullover Fleece Crop Top will give you a near-identical look for $50 less.

Both of the products are available in various colors, from blue to pink.

TikToker @AnnaDani, otherwise known as Anna Danni Brooks, shared the amazing find in her most recent video.

“It’s so soft,” she told her followers. “Look at this detail. That makes the sweatshirt so good.”

The cropped pullover she purchased from Amazon was in a purple shade and had reverse seams that created a similar quality to the more expensive version at Free People.

“It almost looks Free People-esque, but it’s Amazon,” Anna said.

Amazon will match whichever type of look you’re going for, as the item is available in bright, dark, and neutral colors.

“I went brighter for the spring, but you can also get neutral, like a beige color,” Anna said in the video.

And just because you opt to save $50, or 70 percent, that doesn’t mean it lacks quality.

The Amazon shirt has an average rating of 4.1 stars out of five, and many shoppers raved about the item in reviews.

One customer said she’d rate the crop sweater a 10 out of 10.

“Warm, cozy, fits well, the green was even better than expected and I get a million compliments,” the shopper wrote on Amazon’s review page.

Another one said: “This is very soft… I love it so much I bought it in brown as well. Even after washing, it is still soft. I felt very warm and cozy. This is a great buy. I highly recommend.”
