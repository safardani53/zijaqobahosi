Hottest Guys On TV Vampire Diaries &amp; More Supernatural Shows Hollywood Life

 Posted on April 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  364 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

‘The Vampire Diaries’

The hotness on The Vampire Diaries is so powerful, it dates all the way back to Ancient Greece — or so we recently learned. From vampires Damon (Ian Somerhalder) and Stefan (Paul Wesley) to werewolf Tyler (Michael Trevino) and hunter Jeremy (Steven R. McQueen), Mystic Falls is literally crawling with grade-A supernatural man meat. Dig in!

‘The Originals’

With too much hotness for one show to handle, it’s only natural that The Vampire Diaries needed to spin off into The Originals; and what a spin-off it is! We’re invested in the intense story, but we’d also tune in for the sheer man-candy factor alone. Klaus (Joseph Morgan), Elijah (Daniel Gillies) and Marcel (Charles Michael Davis) are almost too easy on the eyes.

‘Teen Wolf’

Is it any wonder the boys of Beacon Hills spend most of their time half-naked? Scott (Tyler Posey), Derek (Tyler Hoechlin), Isaac (Daniel Sharman) and the rest of the Teen Wolf crew are so smokin’ hot, we sometimes feel guilty for watching every week. Then we remember it only looks like porn, and we forgive ourselves.

‘Beauty & The Beast’

We know Vincent (Jay Ryan) is pretty much the lone supernatural stud on Beauty & The Beast, but considering The CW’s sophomore drama has already won several intense tournaments on HollywoodLife.com thus far, we’re thinking Vinnie’s got a pretty good chance of taking this one home all by his himself.

‘True Blood’

Vampires, fairies, fairy-vampires… You never know what you’re going to run into while in Bon Temps, and with True Blood‘s final season airing next summer, you can bet the craziest is yet to come. At least we can count on Bill (Stephen Moyer), Eric (Alexander Skarsgard) and Alcide (Joe Manganiello) to keep things nice and warm for us until it’s time for our last trip into town.

‘Supernatural’

Are angels supposed to be sexy? Well, they are on Supernatural, as are brothers Sam (Jared Padalecki) and Dean (Jensen Ackles), both of whom have a touch of the supernatural in them.

So, HollywoodLifers, which show has the hottest supernatural gentlemen? Vote now!
