Burger chain brings back discontinued item after fans petition and its 'bigger and juicier'

 Posted on July 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  390 words  |  Martina Birk

A BURGER chain with over 500 nationwide restaurants has reintroduced a popular sandwich that was previously discontinued.

It said the new burger is even bigger and juicer than before after fast food fans petitioned to bring it back.

Red Robin has brought back the Burnin’ Love burger, known for its distinctive spiciness and giant jalapeño garnish.

Customers may recognize the sandwich as it was previously available until it was discontinued earlier this year.

The Burning’ Love burger features a beef patty topped with fried jalapeños, salsa, pepper jack cheese, lettuce, tomato, and chipotle aioli on a toasted brioche bun.

This time, the sandwich boasts “bigger, juicier burgers,” with Red Robin claiming via its website: “We upped ourselves on our gourmet burgers.”

It’s no wonder Red Robin felt the need to bring back this beloved sandwich after fans petitioned to reintroduce it.

A campaigner on Change.org wrote: “Nothing compares. Please bring it back.”

“I need it back,” wrote another supporter, who complained the other burgers at Red Robin were not spicy enough.

The Burnin’ Love burger is priced at $15.99 at most stores and available nationwide at participating locations.

The sandwich has numerous customizable options. 

Customers can switch out the beef patty for grilled chicken, crispy chicken, a turkey patty, a veggie patty, or a vegan Impossible patty for a small extra price.

The burger is served with the recognizable Red Robin steak fries, although guests can also switch to various other sides for an added fee.

Red Robin is not the only burger chain that has recently added a spicy new sandwich to its lineup.

Carl’s Jr has pleased fans by announcing the return of the El Diablo burger.

Customers had been begging and petitioning the chain to put it back on the menu since it was discontinued in 2018.

The El Diablo burger features a charbroiled beef patty topped with a fiery habanero sauce, bacon, jalapeño popper bites, and pepper jack cheese.

Meanwhile, Taco Bell is turning up the heat with the reintroduction of the Volcano menu.
