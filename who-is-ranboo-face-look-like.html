Who is Ranboo and what does his face look like?

RANBOO'S fans are wondering what his face looks like behind his signature face mask.

Ranboo joined YouTube on January 30, 2020.

Who is Ranboo and what does his face look like?

Ranboo is an American YouTuber and Twitch streamer.

He is known for his gaming videos involving Minecraft as well as collaborations with other gamers.

Ranboo is a member of the Minecraft server Dream SMP and on YouTube he has almost 45 million views on his videos and almost 3 million subscribers.

While Ranboo's date of birth has not been revealed, he is rumored to be in his late teens. His real name has also not been revealed.

On July 27, 2021, Ranboo began trending on Twitter after reaching 2 million followers.

What does Ranboo's face look like?

In Ranboo's videos, he can be seen wearing dark sunglasses and a face mask.

Fans have been known to compare him to fellow YouTuber Hank Green and for a while many believed that the two could be the same person but Green has since addressed the speculations.

“I see people on Twitter all the time saying Hank Green has Ranboo vibes. What is a Ranboo vibe? Can you guys tell me?” he asked his TikTok fans.

One user replied, “Ranboo vibes I think are just very friendly and a little bit chaotic sorta like a golden retriever?” to which Green responded “Oh this is great news. ”

Following that video, fans quickly crossed off Green as the man behind the mask.

Part of his brand was to keep his identity hidden and remain anonymous on the internet but back in April 2021 part of his face leaked in a video after his mask slipped down while talking.

Ranboo's face can be compared to the average American teenage boy with blonde skater boy hair and hazel eyes.

What is Twitch?

Ranboo has over 1.5 million followers on Twitch.

Twitch is known for being a video streaming service that many gamers use to stream their gaming sessions live to fans who can also watch on the platform.

As of 2020, the streaming service had 3 million broadcasters and 15 million active users.
