KYLIE and Kendall Jenner have joined an array of celebrities in Ariana Grande and Justin Bieber’s new music video Stuck With U.

The clip shows the famous sisters dancing on Kylie’s tennis court at her $36.5 million mansion in Holmby Hills, where they are believed to be quarantining together during lockdown.

Kylie, 22, is dressed in a skintight jumpsuit as she playfully dips sister Kendall, 24, who is wearing a pair of shorts.

A number of Ariana and Justin’s famous friends, including Demi Lovato, Ashton Kutcher and Gwyneth Paltrow, star in the charity music video.

Gwyneth dances with husband Brad Falchuck in their kitchen for their surprise cameo, while Demi shares a kiss with boyfriend, Max Ehrich.

The video – which focuses on what it’s like being ‘stuck’ with those you love in lockdown - also features Ariana’s new love interest, Dalton Gomez.

Appearing to confirm their relationship, the Los Angeles realtor, 27, can be seen kissing the 26-year-old singer in her bedroom towards the end of the clip.

Ariana wears an oversized sweater in the video while her new man does his best to disguise himself in an all black outfit, hiding his face with his hoodie.

Ariana and Dalton have been spending a lot of “one on one” time during quarantine.

A source told E! News: “They run in the same circle. He used to be a dancer and they have many mutual friends.
