Beloved fashion chain abruptly closes all stores with no warning after 'ceasing operations effective

 Posted on May 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  249 words  |  Larita Shotwell

A BELOVED fashion chain has abruptly closed all of its stores with no warning, leaving patrons devastated.

Al's Formal Wear, the popular chain of tuxedo rentals, has closed its doors for good.

The stores, located in Texas, Louisiana, Oklahoma, and Colorado, shuttered their doors for good on Monday, August 7.

Employees were given little warning of the closure, according to ABC 13.

The publication reported that employees received an email that read: "I regret to inform you that you are being laid off from your position effective Monday, August 7, 2023.

"Unfortunately, the company is compelled to cease operations effective immediately."

One groomsman realized the store was closed amid trying to get fitted for a friend's wedding.
