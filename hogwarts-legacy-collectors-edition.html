Hogwarts Legacy Collector's Edition: What does it include?

 Posted on August 11, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  431 words  |  Aldo Pusey

IF you’re a huge Harry Potter fan you may be tempted to buy the Hogwarts Legacy Collector’s Edition.

This special version of the game comes in a large box, with a number of physical and digital rewards to enjoy.

It doesn’t come cheap, but for the most avid fans of the franchise it’s a must buy.

Here’s everything you need to know about the Hogwarts Legacy Collector's Edition.

What comes in the Collector's Edition of Hogwarts Legacy?

The Collector’s Edition of Hogwarts Legacy comes with all of the added benefits of the Deluxe Edition.

This includes the Thestral Mount, Dark Arts Cosmetic Set, and Dark Arts Battle Arena.

More in Hogwarts Legacy

However, it also includes some added in-game extras, such as the Dark Arts Garrison Hat and Kelpie Robe.


Most people are interested in the physical rewards that come with the game, which comes in an ornate collector-style box.

Hogwarts Legacy Collector’s Edition comes with a steel case for you to store your game.

The most exciting extra is the life-size Floating Ancient Magic Wand with Book Base. This is a large open book with a wand that floats above it.

If you pre-order the Collector’s Edition, you will also receive an Onyx Hippogriff mount.

What is included in Hogwarts Legacy Deluxe Edition?

As mentioned above, the Deluxe Edition includes the Thestral Mount, Dark Arts Cosmetic Set, and Dark Arts Battle Arena.

These are in-game items that are exclusive to the Deluxe and Collector’s Editions.

The Deluxe Edition also allows you to access the game three days earlier than standard editions.

Hogwarts Legacy is already out on PS5, Xbox Series X|S and PC.

However, if you want to play on PS4, Xbox One or Nintendo Switch, then you will still gain early access by purchasing these editions.

What is the most expensive edition of Hogwarts Legacy?

The Collector’s Edition is the most expensive edition of Hogwarts Legacy, and is the only edition that comes with physical rewards.

The PS5 and Xbox Series X|S versions have already sold out, and it is unclear if more will be made.

They retail for around £300 ($300), but are now only available through second hand retailers.

However, you can still pre-order the Collector’s Edition for PS4, Xbox One and Nintendo Switch at certain retailers.
