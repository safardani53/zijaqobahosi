TRAVIS Scott looked stoic as he was seen for the first time since Kylie Jenner’s “pregnancy” announcement.

The 30-year-old was spotted walking around New York City on Friday.

Shortly after rumors began to swirl that the rapper’s girlfriend Kylie is pregnant with their second child, Travis was spotted looking serious as he hit the streets of New York.

He wore light wash baggy jeans, a black vintage T-shirt promoting the 1997 Robin Williams movie Flubber and a matching baseball cap.

Travis pulled the look together with a pair of sleek green-and-white Nikes, while he also sported a thin goatee.

The rapper kept his head down during the walk, which marked the first time he had been seen since news broke that the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star is pregnant.

FAMILY OF FOUR?

Following months of speculation that the couple are expecting their second child together, Page Six reported that Kylie is pregnant with baby number two.

The couple already share three-year-old daughter Stormi together.

According to the outlet, the 24-year-old has been opening up about wanting a sibling for Stormi "to multiple sources."

When she asked about having a sibling for Stormi during an interview for Harper’s Bazaar’s March 2020 issue, the makeup guru revealed: “My friends all pressure me about it … They love Stormi. I definitely feel pressure to give her a sibling, but there’s no plan.”

Kylie's father, Caitlyn Jenner, appeared to confirm she is expecting another grandchild to TMZ.

However, the former Olympian did not say who out of her children is pregnant.

A source told The Sun that Kylie Jenner’s due date should be sometime in early 2022, while the insider added the TV personality is pretty early on in her pregnancy. 

DROPPING HINTS

While Kylie and Travis have not yet publicly confirmed the pregnancy, many fans believe makeup mogul has been dropping hints in recent months.

She first sparked pregnancy rumors in late June after she appeared not to take a tequila shot during the KUWTK reunion.

Days later, fans noticed that she was eating sushi that seemed to have no fish at all and just avocado.

However, Kylie had seemingly shut down the rumors by filming herself working out in a tight sports bra and legging sets and drinking wine with friends.

KEEPING SECRETS?

Fans may be be surprised if the pair choose to keep the pregnancy private, as Kylie famously hid her first pregnancy from the public.

It wasn't until after Stormi was born in February 2018 that Kylie shared a YouTube video of her pregnancy trajectory dedicated to her daughter, which announced the big news to fans.

After revealing the news, she explained her decision to keep the pregnancy quiet to Andy Cohen.

"I didn’t know how I would bring that to the public too and have everyone’s opinion. I think it was just something that I needed to go through by myself.”
