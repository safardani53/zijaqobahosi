Football fans all say the same thing as AFC Wimbledon release bizarre blue away kit for 2022-23 seas

 Posted on October 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  325 words  |  Martina Birk

AFC WIMBLEDON have released their new BLUE away kit for their 2022-23 League Two campaign.

But football fans have been left scratching their heads because the Dons' home kit is also... blue.

Revealing the kit on Tuesday, Wimbledon announced on Twitter: "We will don a deep blue and gold @hummel1923 kit away from home this season."

The League Two outfit will wear a snazzy deep blue shirt with gold trimming as they bid for an immediate return to League One under boss Johnie Jackson.

But the only problem is the shirt does not really offer much of an alternative to the traditional blue and yellow home shirts worn by the Wombles.

The main differences are a deeper shade of blue and gold, instead of yellow, trim.
