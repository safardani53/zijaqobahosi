LIVE's Kelly Ripa and Ryan Seacrest have unveiled a change to a fan-favorite part of the show during the first live broadcast of the year.

The pair revealed a new trivia game during Tuesday's episode of Live With Kelly & Ryan, complete with a brand new name.

"So, we have a new game," Ryan announced. "A new trivia game today."

He then raised his hands up in an "air quotes" position and clarified, "It's really the same game."

"It's the same game with a brand new title," Kelly said as she took a sip of coffee.

"Very," Ryan agreed before announcing it was time for Live's "Lucky Day Trivia" instead of "Great Days Trivia" as he threw his hands into the air in excitement.

The pair brought on a viewer named Tammy to answer a trivia question live, spinning the wheel to see what she would be playing for.

Tammy had to answer a question about a location mentioned in an interview the previous day, and ended up winning an all-expenses-paid trip to Waikiki Beach.

Kelly and Ryan excitedly congratulated Tammy on her winnings during their "new" trivia game.

DYNAMIC DANCING

Meanwhile, Live fans have been begging Kelly Ripa to participate in a completely different kind of game.

Viewers have asked Kelly to compete on Dancing With The Stars after the 52-year-old broke out some dance moves in a new video.

Last week, Kelly shared a compilation video where she busted out some dance moves on Instagram.

"Danced my way through 2022," she captioned the video.

She danced alongside an instructor in some scenes and in others could be seen dancing along with a class.

The 52-year-old host showed off turns, high kicks, body rolls and more in time with her instructor, which had fans' jaws dropping.

They commented with a chorus of "DWTS??" and similar requests, clearly impressed by Kelly's moves.

They took to the comment section to compliment Kelly while asking her to join the reality competition series.

Dancing With The Stars' own judge Carrie Ann Inaba was even on board: "I love this and wish I could come and do this with you! How fun!!! Happy new year!!!!"

RYAN'S RUMBLE

While fans begged Kelly to join Dancing With The Stars, host Ryan Seacrest threw some major shade at his New Year's Eve rival, Andy Cohen.

The host called out the Bravo personality on Tuesday's episode of Live with Kelly & Ryan after Andy challenged him to a "brawl."

Kelly had admitted during the pair's first show of 2023 that she had watched both Ryan and Andy's New Year's Eve specials over the weekend.

That prompted Ryan to share some of the behind-the-scenes action while hosting alongside Andy.

"It was funny because my big stage was right in front of Anderson and Andy," Ryan said. "When I was not working, I was trying to get their attention."

Only one of the hosts ended up turning around to say anything to Ryan, and it was Anderson Cooper.

“He turns around and he says, ‘Have a good show.’ Class. Class,'" Ryan explained.

He added that Andy did not turn around, however, a fact that Kelly disputed, saying Andy tried to get his attention instead.

During his time co-hosting CNN's New Year's Eve Live, Andy had challenged the rival hosts with his fists while interviewing musicians and actors Tenacious D.

The pair, Jack Black and Kyle Gass, amped up "beef" between Ryan and Andy, with Andy responding hilariously in kind.

"We're settling it tonight, at midnight, on the main stage."
