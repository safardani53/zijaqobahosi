Rhian Brewster is an English professional footballer who plays as a striker for Sheffield United in the EFL Championship and for the England U21 national team. He began playing football for Chelsea’s youth team in 2008 and continued to do so until 2015. In 2015, he joined Liverpool’s youth team and began his career with the club. He began his career with the club’s U18 team before being moved to the U23 squad, where he scored his first goal against Ipswich Town. He was called up to the first squad for club friendlies in October and November 2016, where he scored a hat-trick against Accrington Stanley. He signed a five-year professional deal with Liverpool in June 2018. On September 25, 2019, he made his competitive debut for the club in an EFL Cup match. On 7 January 2020, he joined Championship team Swansea City on loan for the duration of the 2019-20 season, appearing in 22 games and scoring 11 goals in all competitions. Following his loan spell at Swansea, he returned to Liverpool to begin preseason training ahead of the start of the 2020-21 Premier League season. He signed a five-year contract with Sheffield United on October 2, 2020, for a rumored sum of roughly £23.5 million. The agreement contains a £40 million buy-back clause that is effective until June 30, 2023.

He has represented England at various age levels on the international stage, although he is still eligible to play for Turkey via his Turkish Cypriot mother and Barbados via his Barbadian father. He has played for England at the U16, U17, U18, and U21 levels. He was a member of England’s squad that won the 2017 FIFA U17 World Cup in India, where he was given the Golden Boot for finishing as the competition’s highest goalscorer. On September 6, 2019, he made his England U21 debut in the 2021 Euro qualifying. He has yet to play for England in a senior international match.
