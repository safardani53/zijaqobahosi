Do Dick and Angel Strawbridge get paid for Escape To The Chateau?

 Posted on February 25, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  616 words  |  Larita Shotwell

A LIMITED budget and a huge area of land did not stop couple Dick and Angel Strawbridge from trying to renovate a dilapidated 19th-century French chateau.

But what do they get out of this?

Do Dick and Angel get paid to present Escape To The Chateau?

An exact figure is yet to be revealed, but the show gets almost three million viewers every episode - making it one of Channel 4's most popular series.

Its audience is 50 per cent higher than Channel 4 normally gets in the same show slot. 

The couple also presents the daytime spin-off show Escape To The Chateau DIY - so they must be raking in the big bucks.

Weddings can cost anywhere between £19,000 and £38,000 to book the chateau for your wedding - depending on what package you choose.

The chateau has the potential to host 25 weddings a year - which would equate to £1 million revenue from the weddings alone.

And their TV show - set to air once again on Sunday, October 30, 2022, at 8pm - has only boosted their sales - the couple say they got "a massive spike" in interest and are now fully booked until 2022.

They don't only host weddings.

Angel and Dick sell glamping nights for £350 in "geodesic domes" on the chateau's grounds.

They also offer £230 "days of decadence" where guests get the opportunity to meet Dick and Angel, as well as feast on lunch and afternoon tea at the chateau. 

It doesn't stop there - the couple actually charge guests £75 for a "working day in the garden", with the website saying: "Yes to be frankly honest we are charging you to come and work!

"And in return you have to help make the place beautiful!'"

The chateau is located in the Pays de la Loire region of France.

The journey is a three hours stint from Paris for a shift in the gardens.

The website added further guidelines and put: "There is a degree of manual work expected - though some jobs will be gentler than others - no matter what the weather we will be outside working, so please dress appropriately."

How much did they pay for Château de la Motte-Husson?

The Strawbridge's paid £280,000 when they bought the ruined property 2015.

The immense chateau has 45 bedrooms, 78 windows, 12 acres and one moat.

But when they bought it, the castle had no electricity, heating or running water - but they have transformed it over six years.

It is now reportedly worth approximately £2 million.

The 45-room, five-storey home, Chateau-de-la-Motte Husson, has since been transformed into a stunning home and now into a flourishing business.

Can you buy Escape To The Chateau merchandise?

Dick and Angel sell everything from personalised books, to stationary, to homeware and food and drink.

The couple runs a separate 'Chateau Products' team based in Swindon, Wiltshire to look after all the merchandise inquiries.

Angel Strawbridge also runs her own soft furnishings and wallpaper collection inspired by the Chateau.

What is the couple's net worth?

While the specific net worth of the couple remains unknown, Dick's individual net worth is estimated to be around $3 million, which is equivalent to more than £2 million.

However, in an interview with The Telegraph, Dick insists they are a "normal" couple.

Dick said: "We are very normal and that's the secret; people can see themselves in us.
