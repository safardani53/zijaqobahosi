Kendall Jenner's Jacquemus' Holiday Campaign

 Posted on October 1, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  452 words  |  Aldo Pusey

It’s beginning to look a lot like Ken-mas!

Kendall Jenner channeled classic holiday motifs — from a lit-up spruce to Santa Claus himself — for Jacquemus’ first holiday collection, GUIRLANDE.

The Kardashians star, 28, bundled up — and stripped down — for the Paris fashion house’s latest collection, which it teased on Instagram with a close-up of the model’s lower back, wrapped in a colorful strand of Christmas lights, on Monday.

In a video Jacquemus shared on Tuesday, Jenner tiptoes through colorful lights before donning a Santa-red shearling long coat and skiing down a staircase as Brenda Lee’s “Rockin' Around The Christmas Tree” blares.

The brand cheekily captioned the video, “Don’t try this at home :).”

Elsewhere in the festive new campaign — named after decorative tree garlands — the model dons everything from bulky outerwear to ice skates and skis.

In one photo, she strips down to just a pair of panties and a Santa hat.

Fur and glitter reign supreme in the holiday collection, which adheres to a classic holiday color palette of red and green, silver and gold, and black and white.

GUIRLANDE has every occasion covered. For holiday gatherings, the collection includes several party looks, including a shearling bandeau and miniskirt set and a lurex knit cropped two-piece set in both gold and silver, the latter of which Jenner modeled with matching socks and skates.

The capsule also offers cozy winter staples. From head — a cozy bucket hat and chapka — to toe — fur-lined velvet boots to fuzzy slingback heels — the new pieces promise both warmth and style.

The limited-time collection also features basics like comfy sweaters and T-shirts, all offered in holiday shades like red, white and pine-green.

Classic Jacquemus accessories, including Le Bisou and Le Chiquito, are also available to shop in the capsule collection with new, festive fur details and in new, metallic colorways.

For home, GUIRLANDE also offers a selection of holiday objects — perfect for gifting — from soap and soap dishes to snowball makers to ornament sets — including a set of four modeled after the brand’s most iconic handbags.
