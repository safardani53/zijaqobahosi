Who is Paula Badosa's partner Juan Betancourt?

Juan Betancourt, is a model and actor who's worked with GQ Brazil, GQ Espana, Giorgio Armani, Ralph Lauren amongst others, and also headed a men’s grooming campaign by Tom Ford.

Juan was born on September 22, 1990, in Havana, Cuba, and stands at 6ft 2 inches tall.

He has a net worth of approximately £1.2million and earns money from appearing in films and on TV, as well as modelling.

Who is Paula Badosa?

Paula Badosa Gibert is a Spanish tennis player, whose highest WTA ranking is number two.

Born on November 15, 1997, she made her debut in 2012 at the ITF Women's Circuit and has reached three WTA finals.

Her breakthrough came in 2015 when she received a wildcard at the Premier Mandatory Miami Open where she won her first two matches.

In 2020 she represented Spain in the Summer Olympics.

In 2022, after losing to Victoria Azarenka at the Adelaide International, she went on to gain her third career title in Sydney by beating Barbora Krejčíková.

This took her WTA ranking up to number six.

Badosa got to number two after the April 2022 Porsche Tennis Grand Prix, which took place in Stuttgart.

She said it was "a dream come true".

Paula started dating Juan in 2021, and occasionally posts snaps of the two of them together on her Instagram page.
