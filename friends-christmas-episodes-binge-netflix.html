'Friends' Christmas Episodes to Binge on Max

 Posted on August 29, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  707 words  |  Martina Birk

The one where we watch all the Friends Christmas episodes.

All year round, Max offers every episode of the beloved NBC sitcom. However, if you need a break this holiday season, you can dive straight into the yuletide episodes for a festive binge.

As fans know, Friends Christmas episodes deliver many hilarious and cheerful moments from the show's 10-season run — from Ross (David Schwimmer) and Monica's (Courteney Cox) iconic dance routine to the Holiday Armadillo. There is one per season, with the exception of the first and last seasons.

Keep reading for the complete list.

Season 2, Episode 9: “The One with Phoebe’s Dad”

The series' first Christmas episode follows Monica's attempt to try tipping with cookies as opposed to cash, which doesn't go over so well. As Phoebe (Lisa Kudrow) tries to track down her father, and Ross and Rachel (Jennifer Aniston) have a lovers' quarrel, Joey (Matt LeBlanc) and Chandler (Matthew Perry) forget to buy presents for the gang — and are forced to buy them from a gas station.

Watch “The One with Phoebe’s Dad” on Max

Season 3, Episode 10: “The One Where Rachel Quits”

Yearning for a career in fashion, Rachel quits her job at Central Perk after receiving some bad advice from Joey and Chandler. Phoebe sadly learns that her dead Christmas trees go into the chopper, and Ross accidentally breaks a Girl Scout's (Mae Whitman) leg and is forced to sell the cookies himself to make it up to her.

Watch “The One Where Rachel Quits” on Max

Season 4, Episode 10: “The One with the Girl from Poughkeepsie”

Ross is torn between a woman (Yasemin Baytok) from Poughkeepsie and one who lives nearby. Joey tries to gain Monica good favor at her new job, and things don't go well for Rachel when Chandler sets her up on a bad date. And Phoebe sets out to write a Christmas song for her friends but struggles to get it to rhyme.

Watch “The One with the Girl from Poughkeepsie” on Max

Season 5, Episode 10: “The One with the Inappropriate Sister”

Phoebe decides to give back and collect money for those in need. The three male friends face conflict all living in one apartment, and Monica and Chandler continue to hide their relationship. Rachel, meanwhile, discovers her boyfriend, Danny (George Newbern), is weirdly obsessed with his sister (Julie Lauren).

Watch "The One with the Inappropriate Sister" on Max

Season 6, Episode 10: “The One with the Routine”

Ross and Monica attempt to steal the show at the taping of Dick Clark's New Year's Rockin' Eve, while Joey tries to get a kiss from Janine (Elle Macpherson) at midnight. Back at home, Phoebe, Chandler and Rachel frantically search for Monica's Christmas presents.

Watch "The One with the Routine" on Max

Season 7, Episode 10: “The One with the Holiday Armadillo”

Ross dresses up as an armadillo to teach his son Ben (Cole Sprouse) about Hanukkah. However, Chandler impresses him more when he dresses up as Santa Claus. Phoebe tries to drive Rachel out of Joey's apartment and move back in with her.

Watch “The One with the Holiday Armadillo” on Max

Season 8, Episode 11: “The One with the Creepy Holiday Card”

Ross voices his concerns to Mona (Bonnie Somerville) when she decides to send a Christmas card with both of them on it. Chandler tries to avoid his horrible boss (Sam McMurray), and Rachel — pregnant with Ross' baby — deals with her hormones.

Watch “The One with the Creepy Holiday Card” on Max

Season 9, Episode 10: “The One with Christmas in Tulsa”

Chandler makes a huge decision to quit his job after a co-worker (Selma Blair) tries to make a pass at him. He finally returns home for the holidays, much to the delight of Monica and the rest of his friends. The episode also features various flashbacks.
