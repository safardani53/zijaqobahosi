Gwen Stefani Wants More Kids

 Posted on May 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  290 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Sorry, Kingston James McGregor Rossdale, apparently you’re not enough for mommy.

In an interview with USA Today, Gwen Stefani expressed her affection for her current tyke, along with a yearning to give birth to more.

“I pray that I can have another baby,” Stefani said. “I mean, it’s such a miracle to have one. And there’s so much I still want to do, because who knows? Things could be a lot harder a few years from now. I mean, I’m not at the beginning of my career. I’m on a ticking clock. And I don’t want to miss anything.”

Stefani is friends with Heidi Klum, who has also expressed a desire to provide the world with more beautiful children.

Kingston was born on May 26 in Los Angeles and is already a show-business professional, says his mom.

“There’s this huge team of us always hanging out together, so he gets to see the same people every day,” Stefani said of her entourage of stylists, dancers and others. “And he’s this very cool, chilled-out little guy. He’s just like another person, except that he’s super-cute and super-entertaining.”

He’s also been overshadowed by other celebrity babies in 2006. It’s about time Kingston received some love over Shiloh Nouvel, Suri Cruise and Jayden James Federline!

Meanwhile, Stefani is planning to tour in the spring behind her new album with Kingston in tow. She’s received a lot of credit for losing baby weight so quickly.

“That was one of the hardest things for me,” Gwen said, “the pressure of, ‘OK, I need to get into shape so that I can put this record out.’ If I didn’t have that pressure, I don’t think I would have gotten the baby weight off so quickly.”
