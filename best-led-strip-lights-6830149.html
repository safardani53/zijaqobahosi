How to Pick the Right LED Strip Lights

Length

Look for the right lights that suit your space. Some are as short as 6 inches and are great for shelving, behind TVs, and smaller spaces that you want to highlight. Long LED strip lights can be 100 feet. You may also be able to connect strips together to expand their length. Just make sure that the power supply can support the additional load. Our top pick, the Tenmiro LED lights, spans an impressive 100 feet.

Controls

Many LED strip lights use a remote control or are compatible with a company app that lets you control them without bending down or reaching up to get to a knob or switch. The most advanced, and most expensive, options can be controlled via your smart home device. If you want to be able to change the lights frequently, this is a great feature. For the more budget-conscious, choosing a model that does not have a remote feature can save money.

Settings

If you want to be able to customize your lighting, look for an LED strip light that has multiple modes, colors, music syncing, and a timer. These options often come with a remote control that let you adjust the settings easily. For those using their LED lights for a party, this is a great way to go.

Safety

While LED lights are considered safer than traditional string lights, you should still use caution and common sense when placing your lighting strips around your home or yard. And, look for safety certifications. "LEDs don’t get hot like old-fashioned Christmas lights, and if they come with an electrical plug, they should be labeled as ETL or UL-certified," says Sheva Knopfler, lighting designer and co-founder of Lights.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do you install LED strip lights?

    Most LED strip lights come with an adhesive back. All you need to do is peel off the backing and stick it to a clean, dry surface. Some adhesives are more effective than others. Moisture can make the adhesive wear out faster, as can removing and re-sticking the strip frequently. Usually, you can add a double-sided adhesive to the back when the built-in one no longer works well.

  • What LED strip lights are good for social media?

    LED lights are a great way to add ambiance to your social media photos and videos. Consider if you want natural lighting or something with added color. White lights, especially warm white, can give a flattering glow and make help make your social media feed very tranquil.

  • What are LED tape lights?

    LED tape lights are the same as LED strip lights, called so for the tape adhesive that is on the back to help them stay in place. Most LED strip lights come with an adhesive backing. But not all adhesive is equal and lower quality products may need some new adhesive over time.
