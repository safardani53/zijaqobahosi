A BRIDE-TO-BE has gushed over her growing collection of bridal shoes from the ASOS sale - proving you don’t need to spend a fortune on nuptial accessories. 

Nea Katrina is getting hitched this year but plans to wear a few pairs of shoes across her wedding celebrations. 

So far, she has picked up three sets of white heels from ASOS.

The cheapest of her ivory collection only cost £21 in the e-retailer’s sale. 

The Be Mine Bridal Saniyah heeled sandals with corsage in ivory satin have been reduced from £40 for 2024 brides. 

The glossy heels have been “aisle approved” by ASOS bosses.

They feature an adjustable strap with a pin-buckle fastening, open toe and high-point heel. 

But the star of Scotland-based Nea’s collection is the Be Mine Bridal Cynzia tulle bow detail sandals in ivory.

The glossy heels have their very own veil that drapes over the back of the foot. 

They have been reduced to £24 from £40 for brides-to-be and are available in hues of white, beige or lilac.
