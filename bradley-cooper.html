Bradley Cooper Biography

Bradley Charles Cooper is the full name of Bradley Cooper. He was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States of America, on January 5, 1975. Gloria Cooper, a local NBC affiliate employee, and Charles Cooper, a stockbroker, are his parents. Holly Cooper is the name of his sister.

In 1993, he graduated from Germantown Academy, where he acquired his schooling. Cooper worked for the ‘Philadelphia Daily News’ while attending the university. In 1997, he got a Bachelor of Arts in English with honors from Georgetown University. Bradley earned a Master of Fine Arts in acting from the Actors Studio Drama School in 2000.
