Red Lobster introduces menu items just in time for summer - but there is a catch

 Posted on February 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  422 words  |  Martina Birk

RED Lobster has updated its menu for the summer - and fans already have a wide assortment of crab-centric dishes to choose from.

Starting on June 5, the seafood chain introduced multiple new crab dishes for only a limited time as part of its first Crabfest promotion in four years.

Customers can now order up to a pound of Snow or Bairdi Crab Legs with a choice of three-brand new flavors: Roasted Garlic Butter, Honey Sriracha, or Lemon Pepper.

Crab fans also have the option of getting the crab legs simply steamed.

The crab legs are then served over potatoes with one side.

Additionally, Red Lobster is offering a new surf and turf entree: The Crab & Oscar-Topped Sirloin.

This dish includes a half-pound snow crab with a seven-ounce sirloin steak topped with lump crab served in garlic cream and with a choice of two sides.

Appetizer and dessert fans get two new options, as well.

Customers can now order Crabby Cheese Fries topped with Red Lobster's cheese sauce and lump crab in garlic cream.

And after dinner, Red Lobster is now offering a new Strawberry Cheesecake.

"We've created a menu that gives guests the opportunity to experience Crabfest their way – from choosing from a variety of flavor options to offering delicious appetizer and dessert choices, and of course refreshing summertime sips, it's the perfect way to kickstart the summer season," said Patty Trevino, chief marketing officer at Red Lobster.

The new items will be available at participating locations while supplies last.

Prices will vary depending on location.

NEW DRINKS

Red Lobster also announced new alcoholic drink additions for Crabfest.

For a limited time, fans can order a new Watermelon Martini for just $5 dollars at participating locations.

The cocktail includes New Amsterdam Gin and fresh mint.

They can also pick up a Ruby Mule, which features Deep Eddy Grapefruit Vodka and Q Ginger Beer, with a hint of raspberry flavor.

MORE FOOD NEWS

While Red Lobster is going all in on crab selections for summer, a snack food giant is bringing back a beloved flavor to celebrate the season.

Oreos announced the return of its cotton candy-flavored cookies.

The flavor had been discontinued for nearly a decade before it made its return on June 5.

As with Red Lobster's Crabfest, the Cotton Candy Oreos will only be available for a limited time.
