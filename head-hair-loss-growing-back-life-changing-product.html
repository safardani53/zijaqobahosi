A SOLUTION for hair loss has been revealed by one woman who lived it herself.

She swore by her holy grail product, calling it "life-changing" and demonstrating the results on her locks.

Manrika Khaira (@manrikakhaira) filmed herself discussing the hair hack in a video on TikTok.

She said that she first started to lose the hair on the top of her head due to a brain tumor.

Later on, the balding continued on the sides of her scalp, which she shared pictures of.

"This is like a really, really massive thing for me to show you guys, just because it's my biggest insecurity and I know that there's people on this app that will rip it apart. So this is what's left of my hair," she said.

She filmed herself showing that the once bald spots currently have hair growth.

"I just literally want to show you this growth here and this is all down to rosemary oil," she said.

"I had absolutely nothing, nothing on top. Now I have a parting back. Look how thick and full my hair is on top.

"Hair is still really, really short at the sides here. So I don't really have anything at the sides here to come down with, which is why I can't really wear my own hair down.

"This at the top here is kind of all of this length at the back, it's getting a lot fuller," she added.

Manrika specified that while the hairs are still growing, making them shorter than the rest of her hair, she still praises the product.

"Rosemary oil as a product has changed my whole life," she said.

Rosemary oil by Aliver can be purchased from Amazon for $9.99.

Many viewers asked questions to Manrika on how to best use the product.

"How many times do you use it a week and keep it in for?" one asked.

She replied: "Two [to] three times per week and at least two hours."
