Dave Kindig's Bio: Hobby Turned Passion 

Dave, who celebrates his birthday on 6 February 1960, is a secretive person when it comes to his family life. He has not yet addressed any information about his parents to the media. He pursued his engineering degree in Hot Wheels and Legos.

For Dave, cars were merely a hobby when he was young. He used to draw cars at the age of 5. Self-taught artist, Dave learned from watching others and fixing his own car.

He started building things inside his garage, and everyone started noticing his work. His part-time hobby soon became his full-time work.

He got his breakout job after he was given the opportunity to draw a ‘69 Camaro which got featured in magazines like Super Chevy and Chevy High Performance.

In June 1999, he started Kindig It Design and started drawing and designing cars. His business boomed, and he started getting invited to numerous television shows.
