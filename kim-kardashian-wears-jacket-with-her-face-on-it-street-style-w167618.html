Kim Kardashian Wears Jacket With Her Face On It: Street Style

 Posted on May 12, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  293 words  |  Martina Birk

Her face is all over the place! With a closet full of designer labels and custom clothes, Kim Kardashian has a favorite item: a leather jacket with her portrait painted on it. Since she first wore the piece back in February, Kardashian has opted for the topper on at least three other occasions. 

“I know it’s kinda crazy to wear a jacket with pics of yourself on it, but why not I guess!” the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 35, wrote in a post on her website, kimkardashianwest.com.

The leather garment is actually hand-painted. “The piece is from this sick bespoke brand in New Zealand,” Kardashian said. “The painting on this jacket is so incredible. They did such a good job!”

Most recently, Kardashian rocked “The Selfie Jacket” by Blue Blank over skintight off-the-shoulder midi dress, which she paired with Tom Ford Eyelash shoes, fishnet tights and a ribbon choker on Thursday, March 17.

Kardashian also wore the garment with a lace-up black top, ripped jeans, shades and pink Tom Ford heels while out to a movie with Kanye West on March 12. On that day, she wore her hair in tight pigtail French braids.

The topper (which includes a flashback to her March 2015 blonde lob) was the finishing touch for another outfit on February 24, when she stepped out in a knee-length compression suit and shades. 

The first time the mother of North, 2, and Saint, 3 months, was seen in the custom outerwear, which also has the words “slay” and “chill” on the arms, was at BFF Jonathan Cheban‘s birthday party on February 21. She wore another black scoop-neck midi for the soirée.
