Walmart shoppers rush to buy $73 designer perfume that scans at register with over 40% off

 Posted on April 20, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  316 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

WALMART shoppers have been desperate to buy a designer perfume that is on sale with over 40 percent off.

The retail giant's website regularly sells a number of products across its various departments with special deals not found in-store.

Meanwhile, other shoppers use the store app in order to find hidden deals on shelves.

The online price of the Versace Bright Crystal Eau De Toilette has been slashed by the retailer to under $50.

Originally priced at $73.99, the fragrance is now on sale for just $46.18.

The 3-ounce bottle comes with a classy faux diamond top and has a pink tinge.

The perfume features top notes of yuzu, pomegranate, and water.

Meanwhile, it carries mid notes of lotus flower, magnolia, and peony paired with the base notes of musk, mahogany, and amber.

Versace said it is "inviting and airy, the fragrance is open, inclusive and designed with the Versace woman in mind."

The label adds that it is "a sensual and feminine fragrance, symbolising the Versace woman who is confident and sure of her charm.

"It is a fragrance that reveals juicy yet delicate notes that delight the senses."

One happy Walmart customer wrote on the website: "I love this perfume!

"It has a great blend of sweet floral and musk. My new favorite!"

Another left a five-star review saying: "Beautiful bottle, beautiful smell.

"First time I ever spent this much on perfume it is light and airy smells like blossomed spring flowers. Not loud and strong at all."

Meanwhile, the 3.4-ounce bottle of Versace Eros for Men has been priced down on the Walmart website from $66.57 to $57.99.

According to the website: "Versace Eros is a fragrance for a strong, passionate man, who is master of himself.

"Eros interprets the sublime masculine through a luminous aura with an intense, vibrant, and glowing combination of fresh mint leaves, Italian lemon zest, and green apple."
