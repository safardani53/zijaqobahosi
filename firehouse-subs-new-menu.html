AN iconic sandwich shop has decided to bring back its fan-favorite menu item but with a twist.

Firehouse Subs is bringing back its Pepperoni Pizza Meatball Sub for just $6.

Customers will only be able to purchase the sandwich for a limited time and must use its new app to order.

Firehouse Subs said the iconic sandwich combines three Italian American staples: a classic meatball sub, pepperoni pizza, and garlic bread.

The sub first launched back in 2021 and features Italian meatballs, marinara, extra provolone, crispy pepperoni, and Italian seasoning on a toasted garlic bread roll.

"Guests have been asking for it and we're excited to bring it back," Yosef Hojchman, chief marketing officer at Firehouse Subs, said in a statement.

Just note that the sandwich cannot be combined with any other deal, offer, or coupon.

Additionally, the deal is not valid in-restaurant or on delivery orders, only on the Firehouse Sub Rapid Rescue to Go app.

REWARDS PROGRAM

The Firehouse Sub Rapid Rescue to Go app is free to join, and customers earn 100 points for every $1 spent.

Plus, customers can earn points on up to five eligible transactions from the same account per day.

To use your points, customers must select eligible menu items from participating Firehouse Subs restaurants.

The sandwich chain released a list of eligible menu items and points needed for redemption as of April 18, 2023:

  • 3,000 Points: Dessert
  • 6,000 Points: Small Sub
  • 8,000 Points: Salad
  • 8,000 Points: Medium Sub
  • 10,000 Points: Large Sub
  • 50,000 Points: Deluxe Catering Platter (Website redemption only)

To become a member, head to the Firehouse Sub website and click "sign up."

NEW MENU ITEMS

Firehouse operates more than 1,200 stores across 46 states and also announced another deal back in April.

Earlier this year, the sandwich chain added Smokin’ Triple Stack Sub - a menu item where meat is the star.

For a limited time, customers could grab the sub packed with slow-smoked beef brisket, smoked turkey breast, and Virginia honey ham.

It was also topped with melted cheddar, sweet and tangy slaw, barbecue sauce, and mayo on a toasted sub roll.

Prices start at $7.09 for a small sub here, while the medium and large are available for $11.25 and $16.25, respectively.
