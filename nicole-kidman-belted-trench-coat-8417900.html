’Tis the season for parka jackets, turtleneck sweaters, and all things cozy. But that doesn’t mean you need to sacrifice style for comfort, and Nicole Kidman just reminded us of one particularly elegant coat to break out this winter. 

While filming her upcoming movie Babygirl, the actress was dressed in character in a pair of ballet flats, a shoulder bag, and a long open-front coat in a classic camel color that other stars, including Jennifer Lawrence and Anne Hathaway, have worn recently. 

Her coat had a standout design detail: a tie knot belt. It not only accentuates your body’s shape for a flattering fit, but also dresses up a basic jacket, making it a top choice for occasions like work and dinner dates when you want a more polished look.

If you’re in the market for an everyday coat that has a bit more flair, a belted trench coat is a top choice. We rounded up options from Amazon, Nordstrom, Lulus, and more, and prices start at just $30.

Ladiyo Store Open Front Belted Jacket

It’s no secret that camel and beige-colored clothes are big trends this winter, as the soft hues easily match the various colors you likely already have in your wardrobe. If you want to mimic Kidman’s neutral-colored coat, consider adding this trench coat that’s an Amazon best-seller to your winter clothing rotation. 

The jacket is made with a “soft” and “warm” fabric that one shopper also described as “comfy but not too heavy.” And if you want to bundle up even more, you can secure the belt for a more snug fit. 

Orolay Double Breasted Trench Coat, $85 (Save $35)

A trench coat is forever a staple that celebs keep wearing, too. This double-breasted option by Orolay — a.k.a. the brand behind the viral Amazon coat — is belted like the coat Kidman wore and offers more coverage than an open-front jacket thanks to its buttons that go up the front. 

In addition to an adjustable belt around the waist, it also has two buckle cuffs that you can secure to lock out the cold. Plus, the machine-washable jacket offers two functional pockets to keep your hands protected and warm. Snag it in black, green, red, blue, khaki, and navy.

Lulus Harriet Ivory Button-Front Belted Coat

Last week, Kate Middleton reminded us that black isn’t the only dominating winter hue — there’s winter white. You can get in on the trend with this ivory belted coat by Lulus. 

The jacket has an oversized collar neckline, and it offers optimal warmth, thanks to long sleeves and a long silhouette that falls beneath the waist. The belt cinches your waist, and you’ll find side pockets on the jacket to keep your hands toasty, too. It’s available in two colors, ivory and green, in sizes XS through XL.
