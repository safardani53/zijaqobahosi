Billy Miller's Career: From CSI To GH

Miller came into the spotlight after starring in an episode of CSI: NY in 2006. In 2007, he played the role of Richie Novak, on the ABC Daytime soap opera All My Children. His last appearance as Richie Novak on All My Children paralleled with his first appearance as Billy Abbott on the American series The Young and the Restless in 2008.

He won his first award nomination for supporting actor for his performance in The Young and the Restless in 2010. He succeeded in landing the main prize for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Drama Series in the year and followed it up in 2013 also.

In August 2012, Miller joined as a recurring member on the CW Network’s thriller Ringer.

In March 2018, He won a nomination for an Emmy in the Outstanding Supporting Actor category for his portrayal of Jason Morgan on General Hospital, having joined the show in September 2014.

As of 2018,  he has joined the new series Are You Sleeping, produced for Apple by movie icon Reese Witherspoon; this has caused a massive scare for the General Hospital viewers who are wondering what will happen to his role on GH?

Billy's Bio: Spends Five Years As Patient

Born in Tusla, Oklahoma on September 17, 1979, he grew up in Grand Prairie, Texas. He spent five years of his childhood as a patient in an organization called Texas Scottish Rite Hospital for Children, to which he now supports through various contributions.

He attended Mirabeau B. Lamar High School in Arlington, Texas. Afterward, he graduated from the University of Texas in Austin, Texas with a communication degree. After graduating college, he moved out to Los Angeles where he began working in the mailroom of Industry Entertainment.

Miller screen-tested for the CBS Daytime soap opera As the World Turns at the age of 24. But he lost the audition, and subsequently the management and agency due to lousy management. He took a two-year hiatus from his acting career after that experience. 

During that time, he was supported by his family who encouraged him not to give up and try again. With his family behind him, he went and got the part for an episode in CSY: NY in 2006.
