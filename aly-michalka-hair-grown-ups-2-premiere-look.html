Aly Michalka’s Hair At ‘Grown Ups 2’ — Goes To The Dark Side

Aly first went dark at the beginning of 2013, but her mane was a lighter brown with blonde highlights in the front. The usually curly-haired gal went sleek and straight for the movie premiere, held at AMC Loews Lincoln Square in New York City on July 10.

She channeled old Hollywood glamour with big barrel curls in her shoulder-length mane. She wore black eyeliner and mascara but kept the focus on her pretty pout with a bold red lip. Her skin was smooth and flawless, with her freckles peeking out! Her green eyes looked amazing against her porcelain skin and darker hair.

It’s incredible how different she looks — it truly shows the power of hair and makeup!

Although most girls decide to lighten their locks for the hot summer, we think it’s cool Aly went opposite and is rocking a darker ‘do!
