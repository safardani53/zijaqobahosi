Geordie Shores Marty McKenna asks fans to find a prostitute to service his dad in hospital

 Posted on March 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  387 words  |  Martina Birk

EX-GEORDIE Shore star Marty McKenna has asked his fans to find his sick dad a prostitute who can "service" him in hospital.

The 24-year-old star shared a video from his dad's bedside and wrote over the clip: "Any prozzies for the big man."

Speaking to fans in the video yesterday, he added: "I've got a question for all of yous.

"Does anyone know any prostitutes that would come to a hospital and service me father?"

The reality hunk, who was sensationally sacked from Geordie Shore, panned the camera to his dad, who was propped up with pillows.

His poorly dad told him to "shut up" but Marty added: "Slide into the DMs and let us know, in the Newcastle area."

Last year, The Sun Online revealed Marty had been suspended from Geordie Shore.

A source said at the time: “Marty is just as bad if not worse than Scotty. He parties hard and it can get too much at times. Bosses want him to take some time out to think about the way he’s behaved recently.”

In December, Marty was quizzed by cops following a crackdown in Newcastle which saw four bars closed down.

The reality star was questioned on suspicion of conspiracy to supply Class A drugs.

Marty's request to fans comes after Brighton University was blasted for giving out advice to undergraduates on "turning to sex work" last month.

The uni sparked outrage by encouraging its undergraduates into prostitution by allowing a sex workers' advice stand at its freshers' fair.
