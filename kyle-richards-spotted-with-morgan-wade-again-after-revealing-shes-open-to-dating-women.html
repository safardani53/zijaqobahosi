Kyle Richards spotted with Morgan Wade again after revealing shes open to dating women

 Posted on May 8, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  437 words  |  Martina Birk

Kyle Richards and her rumored ‘GF’ Morgan Wade have faced dating rumors for months. The speculation was denied by The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star after separating from Mauricio Umansky. The public outing comes after Kyle said she was “open to dating women” in a RHOBH trailer.

Kyle Richards and Morgan Wade spotted

Kyle and Morgan were spotted on a lunch date with Kathy Hilton five months after they kissed in a music video. The RHOBH star, wearing a cowboy hat like Wade, then led the group of girls to the valet station.

Richards wore a winter jacket and jeans for the fun outing to La Scala in Beverly Hills on Friday. Adding a pair of black boots and a designer bag to the mix, she paired her look with purple-tinted sunglasses.

Kyle recently said on Amazon Live that she met Morgan after hearing her song on radio. She looked Wade up on Instagram before following her, and then getting a DM from the Bravo star.

RHOBH star is now ‘open to dating women’

Kyle revealed on a RHOBH trailer that she’s “open to dating women” during a lunch with her co-stars. She asked her fellow housewives if they’d consider “dating a woman” – and Dorit Kemsley spits her wine out.

Crystal Kung Minkoff turns the question back onto Kyle, who answers her query with a simple “yeah” and a little nod. In another clip, she speaks to Wade on the phone and says she feels “so anxious.”

“I really don’t know if I can do it,” she continues before the country singer assures her, “You got it.” In this season of RHOBH, Bravo fans even saw Kyle tattooing her initial onto Morgan’s arm.

Inside Kyle and Morgan’s spicy video

Dating rumors hit an all-time high back in August after Kyle joined Morgan in her music video for her single Fall In Love With Me that month, which featured the two women pining for each other.

The video climaxed with a passionate kiss between the two. However, the musician previously admitted that she and Kyle were just trying to “troll the trolls” with the video.

Morgan’s track, Fall In Love With Me, was heard while Kyle wore an emerald flowing robe and red gloves in one scene, and 80s gym wear as she striked a pose in the splits in another.
