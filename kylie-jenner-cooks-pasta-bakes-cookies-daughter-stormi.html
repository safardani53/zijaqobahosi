KYLIE Jenner cooked and baked a feast for her 3-year-old daughter Stormi including delicious pasta and homemade cookies.

The Keeping Up With The Kardashians star has raised pregnancy speculation through her diet and clothing choices of late.

Kylie, 23, took to Instagram on Sunday to share her favorite cookie recipe and show off her cooking skills with fans.

The reality star videotaped herself mixing together flour with several other ingredients as she whisked the dry contents into two sticks of butter.

The TV personality's daughter Stormi, who she shares with her boyfriend Travis Scott, made sure to wash her hands so she could help her mom with the preparation.

After adding sugar to the other ingredients, Kylie mixed the contents into cookie dough by adding eggs, vanilla extract, and "folding" in chocolate chips.

The mother of one then set aside her dough to cool while she chopped vegetables and put them on heat to sauté.

The Kylie Cosmetics founder chopped up fresh tomatoes and cucumbers from her garden, while the cookies began to bake in the oven.

Kylie then snapped a photo of a delicious pasta dish she created with her home-grown tomatoes and basil.

"Made the sauce from the tomatoes and basil from my garden!!! This makes me so happy," she captioned the picture.

Later, the influencer showed off the final product as she enjoyed dinner with Stormi.

The mom-and-daughter duo ate the pasta with a fresh cucumber salad and cookies for dessert.

BABY ON THE WAY?

Kylie's feast followed ample speculation that she and Travis, 29, are pregnant with their second child.

However, she seemed to squash rumors on Saturday when she posed in a colorful skin-tight see-through dress.

The TV star snapped a photo in the long-sleeved pink and orange outfit with abstract art detail, and she told fans she was in "fittings."

Her figure looked perfect and her tummy looked flat despite fan conspiracies that she could be pregnant.

In early July, a source told The Sun that Kylie and Travis were trying for another baby, but that they weren't expecting quite yet.

"Kylie and Travis are doing great. They are in the best place they’ve been in years," they explained, after the couple's reconciliation in April.

"She is not pregnant, though they have been trying for baby number two for the past few months. They aren’t engaged at this point, either."

The insider also added that Travis has always been a "hands-on dad" to Stormi and will be the same way with another child.
