It’s officially December, the season of gifting, and while presents under the tree are exciting, stocking stuffers are the unsung heroes of the holiday. 

There’s nothing better than opening up fun little odds and ends like candy, beauty products, a fuzzy pair of socks, or Apple AirTags to keep track of your necessities. If you’re looking for some ideas to add to your loved ones' holiday stockings this year, Target has you covered with a curated stocking stuffer shop, and prices start at just $2. 

Keep scrolling for our top 50 picks, including Thinking Putty, Squishmallows, fizzy bath bombs, and cozy mittens.
