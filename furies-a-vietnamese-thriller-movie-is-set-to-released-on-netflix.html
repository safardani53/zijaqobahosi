Furies A Vietnamese Thriller Movie Is Set To Released On Netflix

 Posted on March 31, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  500 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Furies, Netflix’s new Vietnamese horror film, is set to premiere on the streaming platform on Thursday, March 23, 2023. The film, a precursor to the famous 2019 film Furie, examines Vietnam in the 1990s, concentrating on the lives of three highly determined vigilantes resolved to bring down a renowned criminal organization. Veronica Ngo plays one of the main characters in the film, and several others play important secondary characters. She is also the film’s producer and co-writer.

The Furies teaser from Netflix promises a lot of excitement and conflict.

On February 28, 2023, Netflix released the official teaser for Furies, which gives a glimpse into the perilous lives of the film’s three major characters. The video begins with a narration that establishes the tone of the film. It states,

”Have you ever questioned whether what we’re doing is correct?”

The video then quickly depicts several brutal action scenes from the film, but no significant details are revealed. Overall, it retains a very somber tone that fans of vengeance action movies will undoubtedly enjoy. According to Netflix’s YouTube account, here is the original summary of the film:

In this precursor to “Furie,” three angry vigilantes band together to take down a menacing criminal organization that rules the mean streets of Saigon in the 1990s. According to the video and description, fans can expect an extremely enjoyable and riveting action drama that delves into a variety of complicated topics such as vigilantism, crime, and vengeance, among others.

Furie, a 2019 picture, recounts the tale of a mother who sets out to find her daughter’s captors. The film was a financial and critical success, with many reviewers applauding main actor Veronica Ngo’s performance, as well as the script and graphics.

Who is the cast of Furies? Details about the cast were investigated.

As Hai Phuong, Veronica Ngo plays the protagonist in Furies. Phuong is a strong-willed lady who isn’t scared to face even the most hazardous offender. She’s a former criminal who went on a terrifying quest to find her daughter’s kidnappers in the previous picture. Ngo appears fantastic in the teaser and promises to give an engaging performance in the film. Her portrayal in the 2019 picture garnered broad critical praise, and it will be fascinating to see how her character is developed in the forthcoming spinoff.

Aside from the Furie films, Veronica Ngo has appeared in a number of TV programs and films over the years, including Da 5 Bloods, Going Home for Tet, and The Princess, among others. Dong Anh Quynh, who plays the character of Bi in the film, co-stars with Ngo in another important part. There aren’t many other specifics about her character at this time, but she’s anticipated to play a significant part.

YOLO the Movie and Dien Tho Imperial Palace’s Homicide Cases are among her other noteworthy performing accomplishments. Toc Tien, Rima Thanh Vy, and many others round out the ensemble. Don’t neglect to view Furies, a suspense film, on Netflix on Thursday, March 23, 2023.
