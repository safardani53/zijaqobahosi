Who are the Baldwin brothers?

 Posted on August 20, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  397 words  |  Martina Birk

THE Baldwin family certainly has a few famous faces in it.

Alec Baldwin has three brothers in the industry: Stephen, Bill and Daniel.

Who are the Baldwins?

All of the Baldwin brothers work in the entertainment industry.

Born to mother Carol Newcomb Baldwin and father Alexander Rae Baldwin, Jr., the famous brothers were raised in a Catholic family of French and Irish descent.

The brothers also have two sisters: Jane Sasso and Elizabeth Keuchler.

Who is Alec Baldwin?

Alec Baldwin rose to fame while appearing on the sixth and seventh seasons of the CBS primetime soap opera Knots Landing.

The actor went on to appear in numerous films, including Beetlejuice (1988), Working Girl (1988), Married to the Mob (1988) and Talk Radio (1988).

After gaining attention for his roles in The Hunt for Red October (1990) and in Glengarry Glen Ross (1992), Alec starred in Alice (1990), To Rome with Love (2012) and Blue Jasmine (2013), The Aviator (2004) and The Departed (2006).

On the TV side, Alec is known for his role as Jack Donaghy on the popular NBC sitcom 30 Rock opposite Tina Fey.

Additionally, Alec received critical acclaim for his portrayal of Donald Trump on Saturday Night Live.

He won his third Primetime Emmy award for the role in 2017.

Who is Daniel Baldwin?

Daniel works as an actor, director and producer.

He may be best known for his portrayal of Detective Beau Felton on the NBC series Homicide: Life on the Street.

Daniel also starred in the films Ned Blessing: The True Story of My Life (1992), Mulholland Falls (1996), Vampires (1998), The Pandora Project (1998), Stealing Candy (2002), Paparazzi (2004) and Grey Gardens (2009).

From 2017 to 2019, Alec's brother hosted The Daniel Baldwin Show on radio station WTLA in Syracuse, New York.

Who is Bill Baldwin?

Bill is the second youngest Baldwin brother.

He is best known for acting in the films Flatliners (1990), Backdraft (1991), Sliver (1993), Virus (1999) and The Squid and the Whale (2005).

Additionally, he played the memorable role of William Van Der Woodsen for 11 episodes of the original Gossip Girl from 2010-2012.

The actor married wife Chynna Phillips in 1995.

Bill and Chynna share three children together.

Who is Stephen Baldwin?

Stephen is the youngest of the Baldwin brothers.

The actor is best known for appearing in films such as Born on the Fourth of July (1989), Posse (1993), 8 Seconds (1994), Threesome (1994), The Usual Suspects (1995), Bio-Dome (1996) and The Flintstones in Viva Rock Vegas (2000).
