Woman shares recipe for a Drumstick Squashies cocktail - and its perfect for the weekend

 Posted on July 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  247 words  |  Larita Shotwell

A WOMAN has shared her recipe for a Drumstick Squashies cocktail and it is perfect for the weekend.

Taking to the Facebook group, Extreme Couponing & Bargains UK, she shared the simple five ingredient recipe.

She wrote: "I love these sweets so much I created an alcoholic drink version."

To perfect the drink you will need Apple and Cherry Sourz, vodka, apple and cherry syrup, lemonade, as well as the sweets to top them off (full ingredients below).

She simply blends the Apple Sourz, vodka and apple syrup with ice so that it forms a slushie-like consistency.

Top up with the Cherry Sourz, cherry syrup, and then pour over ice and finish with lemonade and the sweets.

Fellow group members were seriously impressed with her creation, eager to try it for themselves.

One wrote: “I’m gunna start cocktail making again lol”

“This looks and sounds fab!” added another, with a third commenting, “Mmmmm mouth watering now.”

Drumstick Squashies cocktail

  • 35 ml Apple Sourz
  • 25 ml vodka
  • Dash of apple Monin syrup
  • Ice
  • 25 mls Cherry Sourz
  • 20 mls Monin cherry syrup
  • Lemonade
  • Squashies Cherry & Apple
