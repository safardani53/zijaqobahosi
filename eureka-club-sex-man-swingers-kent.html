Hundreds of sex-mad swingers set to descend on sleepy Kent village for wife-swapping festival comple

 Posted on April 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  362 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

HUNDREDS of sex-mad swingers will descend on a historic village for a Bonk Holiday weekend of wife-swapping.

They will hot-foot it to a popular sex resort nestling in a pretty rural village in Kent.

The exclusive Eureka naturist club – where membership costs £400-a-year and couples pay £35 to attend parties – dedicated last night’s party to a “cougars and cubs” free-for-all.

Today there will be a foam party and outdoor hot-tubs for swingers who want to romp in the open-air.

And on Sunday there’s a Hawaiian themed beach party night with fancy dress and a band.

The club is set in 23 acres of parkland and also has a 60ft swimming pool and a giant sauna with room for 25 people.

It even has lodges and cabins on the estate for exhausted swingers to stay over at up to £60-a-night.

The club is in the posh village of Fawkham in Kent, which has a 12th-century church and is close to Brands Hatch race track.

Eureka hosts regular parties and fetish nights featuring whipping and special events for bisexuals, gays and crossdressers.

The club’s website promises: “All of the club facilities are open during our events so you can use the Jacuzzis, sauna, steam room or showers to relax.

“We do not have a strict dress policy however please no street wear or scruffy work wear, also ladies no jeans please.”
