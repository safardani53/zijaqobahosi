Kim Hyun-joong Revealed His Engagement During His Concert

 Posted on October 4, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  610 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Kim Hyun-Joong, a Korean star turned actor, revealed the news of his wedding in an unusual manner after days of media speculation. The actor was thought to marry his longstanding non-celebrity partner but told the announcement during a recent performance. According to rumors, the couple plans to forego their wedding ceremony owing to the continuing COVID-19 outbreak.

Kim Hyun-Joong confirmed his engagement.

Kim Hyun-Joong presented an online and offline concert titled “What I Want To Say” on February 27. While entertaining the crowd with his songs, the star also stunned them by announcing his engagement. Following the show, the actor-singer announced the news, saying:

“I’m not sure how to tell you initially. I made the choice to go through the rest of my life with the one who stood by me through the most tough and trying circumstances.”

The actor from Boys Over Flowers went on to say:

“When I reflect on the past, I’ve received a lot of attention and love up to now, and while I’m sincerely grateful to all the fans who silently stood by my side during the dark and difficult days, I believe today will be a meaningful day because we’ll meet face-to-face and I’ll be able to talk to you. When the dark and tough days progressed, I was able to find confidence and chose to restart the second part of my life owing to my fans’ love, support, and trust.”

Kim Hyun-Joong went on to clarify that he did not wish to announce his engagement by a simple letter or post, as many other celebrities have done. The actor wanted to personally thank his fans for years of support by announcing a new chapter in his life.

Soon after, Kim Hyun-agency, Joong’s HENECIA, issued a press release addressing the rumors. They stated:

“The announcement of our musician Kim Hyun Joon’s marriage was publicized today during his performance. They have bypassed the ceremony and other processes due to the severe [pandemic] circumstances, and we sincerely urge that you abstain from undue speculation as we have carefully relayed the news in consideration of the newlyweds.”

The agency urged the public to continue supporting the actor, adding:

“Warm wishes as [the pair] takes the first cautious step toward a future they will walk together as a cherished partnership at a tough time.”

Kim Hyun-Joon first became public attention in 2005, when he made his debut with the K-pop group SS501. He later began acting, his most prominent part being Jihoo in the blockbuster drama Boys Over Flowers. Kim Hyun-album Joon’s HAZE was released in November.

All About Kim Hyun-joong

Kim Hyun-joong is a well-known actor and singer from South Korea. He is renowned for being the lead and significant rapper of the South Korean boyband SS501. His plays ‘Playful Kiss’ and ‘Boys Over Flowers’ are well-known. Kim is famous in Korea and is regarded as a fashion icon. His Japan tour was a huge success, and he gained a lot of popularity. Even though he has been involved in multiple legal disputes, his celebrity has not suffered, and his admirers continue to adore him. Among his notable albums are ‘Unlimited,’ ‘Heat,’ ‘Tonight,’ ‘Round 3’, and ‘Imademo.’ He has been on television series such as ‘Inspiring Generation,’ ‘Can Love Be Refilled,’ and ‘Nonstop 5’. He has also starred in series like ‘Happy Together 3’, ‘K-Pop Star Conquering The World,’ ‘We Got Married,’ and many more. He provided a character’s voice in the film ‘Shark Bait / Pi Story.’
