Heidi Montag Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

 Posted on September 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  309 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Bio:
Heidi Montag (born Heidi Blair Montag on September 15, 1986 in Crested Butte, Colorado) is an American television personality, singer, fashion designer and author. Heidi grew up in Colorado, graduating high school to attend the Academy of Art University in San Francisco for one semester. During freshmen orientation, she befriended Lauren Conrad, the primary cast member of Laguna Beach: The Real Orange County, a MTV reality series. Heidi was featured in four episodes of the second season. In 2006, she was cast as part of the primary cast of the Laguna Beach spin-off, The Hills. In 2007, she began dating Spencer Pratt, leading to a falling out between Heidi and Lauren. Spencer was added to the cast and the two, dubbed “Speidi,” remained until 2010. Following the end of The Hills, Heidi began acting, making her first feature film debut in 2011’s Just Go With It. She also worked on Famous Food, a VH1 reality competition. In 2013, she and Spencer competed on the British version of Celebrity Big Brother, coming in second to Rylan Clark. They appeared on Celebrity Wife Swap in 2014, and Marriage Boot Camp in 2015. Heidi also has a music career, releasing her first EP in 2009 and following it up with her album, Superficial, in 2010.

Best Known For:
Heidi Montag is best known as a reality television personality, appearing on such shows as The Hills and Celebrity Big Brother.

Personal Life:
In 2008, Heidi and Spencer eloped in Mexico. A year later, they formally married. In 2010, she filed for divorce from Spencer, citing “irreconcilable differences,” but confessed this was a publicity stunt to help her career. Heidi has been open with her multiple cosmetic surgery operations, admitting that she once underwent 10 procedures in a single day. Heidi and Spencer announced they were expecting a baby on April 12, 2017.
