Considering the number of responses we’ve received to a Patrice Hollis interview from a couple months back, The Hollywood Gossip staff has decided to profile another Playboy Playmate. Say hello to Monica Leigh.

Leigh rose to fame as Playboy’s Cyber Girl of the Week for August 29, 2005. From there, it was on to Cyber Girl of the Month for December 2005; Playboy Playmate in March 2006; and finally the 2006 Cyber Girl of the Year later that month.

Kimberly Bell, who poses naked in this month’s issue of the magazine, can only hope to achieve such lofty status someday.

And why did Leigh enter into this business? She’s been friends with Courtney Rachel Culkin (Miss April 2005) since the pair were 12 years old.

Unlike Heidi Montag and Lauren Conrad, they’ve remained tight.
