Ronaldo de Assis Moreira, better known as Ronaldinho Gaúcho or simply Ronaldinho, is a former Brazilian professional soccer player and currently Barcelona's ambassador. He plays primarily in attacking midfield, but is also deployed in winger. Often regarded as one of the best players of his generation and regarded by many as one of the greatest of all time, Ronaldinho has won two FIFA Player of the Year awards. and a Golden Ball. As a global icon in the sport, he is known for his technical skills, creativity and dribbling abilities, as well as the use of tricks, long shots, high shots, Blind pass and free kick accuracy. Ronaldinho made his first career debut for Grêmio in 1998. At the age of 20, he moved to Paris Saint-Germain in France before signing for Barcelona in 2003. During his second season at Barcelona, he won his first FIFA World Player award. Barcelona won the La Liga 2004-05 championship.

Soccer Player Ronaldinho was born in Porto Alegre, Brazil on March 21, 1980. He's 43 years old today.

After making his debut for the Brazilian national team in 1999, he quickly rose to prominence. From 2003 until 2008, he was a member of Team FC Barcelona and was crowned FIFA World Player of the Year in 2004-05.
