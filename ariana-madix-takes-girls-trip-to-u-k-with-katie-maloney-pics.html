Ariana Madix Takes Girls Trip to U.K. With Katie Maloney: Pics

 Posted on May 2, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  518 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Spicing up her life! Ariana Madix left the drama in California as she headed on a girls trip to the U.K.

The Vanderpump Rules star was in Cheshire, England, with Katie Maloney, Dayna Kathan and Raleigh Seldon on Saturday, April 22. The quartet traveled across the pond for the wedding of their pals James Baker and Courtney Berman.

“Lads on tour 🇬🇧,” Madix, 37 captioned a couple of snaps from the event.

James Kennedy commented, “The lads! 😂👏🔥🇬🇧🇬🇧🇬🇧🇬🇧🏴󠁧󠁢󠁥󠁮󠁧󠁿🏴󠁧󠁢󠁥󠁮󠁧󠁿🏴󠁧󠁢󠁥󠁮󠁧󠁿,” while Maloney, 36, added, “She’s the lady of the manor!”

The Lifetime actress wore a bronze gown with spaghetti straps and nude sandals to the event. She also wore a beige peacoat over her look amid the chilly English weather.

Maloney, meanwhile, shared a similar photo of the four women in front of a red double decker bus. “Spice World: Lads on Tour,” she captioned the pic, referring to the iconic 1997 film starring the Spice Girls.

The women posed for a mirror selfie and showed off their fabulous footwear via Instagram Stories.

The British adventure comes just one week after Madix hit Coachella in Indio, California. She was seen packing on the PDA with trainer Daniel Wai over the weekend and they packed on the PDA at LAX airport a couple of days later.

While the Florida native and the fitness guru — who is based in New York — enjoyed their time together, multiple sources exclusively told Us Weekly that Madix was “enjoying her freedom and being single for the first time in years.”

“She is allowed to date whoever she wants and was having an amazing time at Coachella with Daniel,” the insider told Us. “She’s definitely open to seeing where things go with Daniel, but at the same time, Ariana isn’t totally ready to jump into a serious relationship.”

Madix is newly single after ending her nine-year relationship with Tom Sandoval. The Fancy AF Cocktails authors called it quits in March after Madix learned the TomTom co-owner, 40, was having an affair with their Pump Rules costar Raquel Leviss.

Sandoval, for his part, wished his ex-girlfriend and Wai well after their Coachella PDA went viral. “Yes, I love that,” he told TMZ on Tuesday, April 18. “Yes, I do [want her to find love again]. I really do.”

Earlier this month, the Tom Sandoval and the Most Extras frontman alleged that he tried to break up with Madix on Valentine’s Day. “She’s like, ‘I’m not letting you leave me. You’re going to have to force me out of this relationship.’ And I was like, ‘Ariana, I don’t think I could be faithful in a relationship with you right now.’ I was saying all of these things. I hadn’t told her about Raquel yet,” he claimed on the “Howie Mandel Does Stuff” podcast. “She was like, ‘If you breakup with me, you will effectively be ending my life.’ …. I didn’t know what to do. She was completely in denial and not accepting it.”
