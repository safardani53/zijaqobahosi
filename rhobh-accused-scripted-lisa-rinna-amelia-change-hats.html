REAL Housewives of Beverly Hills fans are accusing the show of being ‘scripted.’

The allegations follow Lisa Rinna’s daughter Amelia appearing to change hats between shots.

The Real Housewives Franchise Instagram shared another post which read: "Why does Rinna's daughters have 2 different hats during their 'free-flowing' conversation... It's literally one second later...Scripted ass crap #RHOBS #RHOBH."

The fan account captioned the post: "RHOBH: Viewers of last nights RHOBH episode have caught Bravo re-filming a scene between Lisa Rinna and her daughter, Amelia Hamlin #realhousewives #rhobh"

This week's episode saw Lisa chat with her daughter, Amelia Gray Hamlin, about her dancing Instagram videos. New cast member, Garcelle Beauvais, had asked if these videos played any part in Amelia's eating disorder.

Also during the scene, Amelia’s hat changed or was seen in different spots, leading fans to question if the scene was reshot or overly scripted.

Fans weighed in on the scene's inconsistencies: "Her daughter couldn’t even keep a straight face during her conversation with her mother about her dancing on instagram! Definitely scripted! Ridiculous"

Other wrote: "They’re a lil sloppy with the editing in quarantine"

Another commented: "I noticed that too. So staged"

Others speculated: "How many takes? She clearly swapped hats before they started to have the conversation... the hat wasn’t flip floppin all conversation.. you see the hat on the hat rack at the start of the scene.. I don’t think we need to see her changing hats to get the gist it the scene.."

However one questioned if it was all just an honest mistake: "Although maybe not as heavy with implication as this scene, but, Rinna’s to-camera confessionals at the end of the episode also seemed dodgy. In one piece, her Vogue book on the coffee table was facing spine to camera. Next scene, bottom end to camera. Another re-record to change her take? Maybe just a mix up"
