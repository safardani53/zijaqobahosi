Jeffrey Marshall Foxworthy is an American comedian, actor, writer, producer, television personality, radio and author. He is a member of the Blue Collar Comedy Tour, a comedy troupe that includes Larry the Cable Guy, Bill Engvall and Ron White. Known for a single "You Could Be a Redneck", Foxworthy has released six major label comedy albums. His first two albums were certified 3 × Platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America. Foxworthy has written a number of books based on his jokes, as well as an autobiography titled No Shirt, No Shoes ... No Problem! Foxworthy has also worked on a number of television projects, starting in the mid-1990s with his own sitcom called The Jeff Foxworthy Show. He has also appeared with Engvall and Larry the Cable Guy on several Blue Collar TV specials, including Blue Collar TV for WB. Since 2007, he has been running a quiz.
